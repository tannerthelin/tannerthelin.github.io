About Me

I'm a visual designer and UX/UI enthusiast living in in Provo, Utah. I spent the last two years living in New York City, designing stuff at Greenhouse. Born and raised in Utah, I initially got swept up in design via small logo, poster, and branding projects. Over time, I came to realize that I wanted to solve real problems, and create meaningful experiences for users.

I have been designing professionally for close to 7 years, working with companies like Moki and SolutionStream. I believe that the best products are well-designed inside and out, from the naming of variables and the file architecture to the copywriting and the visuals. Everything matters.

In my spare time, you can find me running, watching old episodes of The Office, and practicing the piano. I’m passionate about anything related to social reform, environmental change, and the arts. If you’re interested in working with me, check out my résumé or LinkedIn, then email me and let’s get talking.

Cheers,
